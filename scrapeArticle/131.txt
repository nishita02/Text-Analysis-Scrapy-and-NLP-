Title: ['What are the key policies that will mitigate the impacts of COVID-19 on the world of work?']

China, which felt the primary impact of the pandemic, was an early mover during this space. As home to a number of the world’s largest firms, it offers lessons for people who are just commencing to embrace the shift.
